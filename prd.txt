About the Conference


Lincoln University College, Nigeria, in collaboration with **********proudly presents the 1st International Conference on Sustainable Healthcare and Health Systems Management (ICSHSM 2026).


ICSHSM 2026 aims to serve as a premier global platform bringing together academicians, healthcare professionals, policymakers, researchers, and industry experts to explore innovative and sustainable solutions in healthcare delivery and health systems management. In an era marked by rapid technological advancements, increasing healthcare demands, and global health crises, the need for resilient, efficient, and sustainable healthcare systems has become more critical than ever.


Under the theme “Advancing Sustainable Healthcare: Innovation, Resilience, and Global Health Systems Transformation,” the conference will focus on transformative approaches that integrate technology, policy, management, and patient-centered care to strengthen healthcare systems worldwide. The conference emphasizes sustainability, equity, accessibility, and quality in healthcare delivery.


ICSHSM 2026 encourages interdisciplinary collaboration and knowledge exchange through keynote sessions, plenary discussions, research presentations, and networking forums, aiming to generate actionable insights that contribute to building robust and future-ready healthcare systems.


We warmly invite participants to contribute to this global dialogue and be part of shaping the future of sustainable healthcare.
Conference Objectives


* To promote innovative and sustainable healthcare practices


* To explore advancements in health systems management and policy


* To foster collaboration between academia, industry, and healthcare institutions


* To address global challenges in healthcare accessibility, quality, and equity


* To provide a platform for sharing cutting-edge research and practical solutions


Conference Scope / Themes


The conference will cover, but is not limited to, the following areas:


    Sustainable Healthcare Systems


* Green healthcare practices and environmental sustainability


* Climate change and health systems resilience


    Health Systems Management


* Healthcare leadership and governance


* Strategic planning and performance management


    Digital Health and Innovation


* Artificial Intelligence in healthcare


* Telemedicine and digital transformation


    Public Health and Global Health


* Pandemic preparedness and response


* Health equity and access


    Healthcare Quality and Patient Safety


* Quality improvement frameworks


* Patient-centered care models


    Health Economics and Policy


* Healthcare financing and resource allocation


* Policy development and evaluation


    Integrative and Complementary Medicine


* Holistic and personalized healthcare approaches
